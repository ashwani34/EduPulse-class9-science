Chapter 5 | Biology

Summary

This chapter is about the basic structural and functional unit of life, the cell. It covers the discovery, types (prokaryotic vs. eukaryotic), differences between plant and animal cells, cell organelles, membranes, and nucleus.

‚Ä¢ Important Cell Organelles

[Image of a labeled Eukaryotic Cell]

1. Plasma Membrane

Selective permeability ‚Üí controls entry & exit of substances. Helps in osmosis and diffusion.

2. Nucleus

Controls all cell activities; contains DNA (chromosomes).

3. Mitochondria

Powerhouse of the cell (releases energy through cellular respiration).

4. Golgi Apparatus

Sorting, packaging, and secretion of materials.

5. Ribosomes

Protein synthesis.

6. Vacuoles

Storage of food, water, and waste. Large and permanent in plant cells.

7. Endoplasmic Reticulum (ER)

Transport system. Rough ER (with ribosomes) and Smooth ER (lipid synthesis).
