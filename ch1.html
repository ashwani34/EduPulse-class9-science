Matter in Our Surroundings

Everything is Matter

Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. It is the building block of our universe.

01

Physical Nature of Matter

Particulate Nature

Matter is not a continuous block; it's made of tiny particles. Think of a sandcastle made of individual grains.

Tiny Scale

These particles are so small they are beyond our imagination. A single drop of water has billions of them!

02

States of Matter

Solids

  • • Fixed shape & volume
  • • Negligible compressibility
  • • Strong force of attraction

Liquids

  • • Fixed volume, no fixed shape
  • • They flow (fluids)
  • • Moderate attraction

Gases

  • • No fixed shape or volume
  • • Highly compressible
  • • Weakest attraction

Evaporation Cooling

Why do we wear cotton in summer? Evaporation absorbs heat from our body, leaving us feeling cool. This is a surface phenomenon.
